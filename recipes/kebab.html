Kebab Ingredients:

  • Minced meat (beef, lamb, chicken, or mixed)
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Ginger, grated or minced
  • Fresh coriander leaves (cilantro), chopped
  • Mint leaves, chopped
  • Green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • Garam masala
  • Ground cumin
  • Ground coriander
  • Red chili powder (optional, adjust to taste)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Gram flour (besan), optional (to bind)
  • Lemon juice
  • Oil, for brushing

Steps to Make Kebabs:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine minced meat, finely chopped onion, minced garlic, grated ginger, chopped coriander leaves, chopped mint leaves, and chopped green chilies.
  2. Add garam masala, ground cumin, ground coriander, red chili powder (if using), and salt. Mix well to combine all ingredients evenly.
  3. If the mixture is too wet, add gram flour (besan) gradually to bind the ingredients together.
  4. Add lemon juice to enhance the flavors, mix well again.
  5. Cover and refrigerate the mixture for at least 1 hour to let the flavors meld.
  6. Preheat the grill or oven to medium-high heat. If using skewers, thread the marinated meat mixture onto skewers, shaping them into kebabs.
  7. Brush the kebabs lightly with oil.
  8. Grill or bake the kebabs, turning occasionally, until cooked through and nicely browned on all sides.
  9. Once cooked, remove from heat and let them rest for a few minutes.
  10. Serve hot kebabs with mint chutney, yogurt dip, or your favorite sauce.
